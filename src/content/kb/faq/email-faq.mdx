# Email Gateway FAQ

## General

**Q: What email protocols does the gateway support?**
A: SMTP for inbound/outbound scanning. POP3/IMAP monitoring is supported in transparent mode.

**Q: Can I use the Email Gateway with Microsoft 365?**
A: Yes. Configure the gateway as an MX relay in front of Microsoft 365 for inbound filtering.

**Q: What is the maximum email processing capacity?**
A: Depends on model. The NG-3000 processes up to 500,000 emails per hour.

## Anti-Spam

**Q: What spam detection methods are used?**
A: Multi-layer detection including reputation checks, content analysis, Bayesian filtering, sender verification (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), and machine learning.

**Q: Can users manage their own quarantine?**
A: Yes. End-user quarantine portal allows users to review, release, or delete quarantined messages.

**Q: How do I whitelist a sender?**
A: Navigate to Policy > Anti-Spam > Whitelist and add the sender email or domain.

## Anti-Virus

**Q: How many AV engines are used?**
A: Dual-engine scanning with real-time cloud sandbox for zero-day threats.

**Q: Are encrypted attachments scanned?**
A: Password-protected archives are flagged. Known password patterns can be configured for automatic extraction.

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Legitimate emails are being marked as spam. What should I do?**
A: Check sender reputation, add to whitelist, and review spam score thresholds under Policy > Anti-Spam > Sensitivity.

**Q: Emails are delayed. What should I check?**
A: Review mail queue under Monitoring > Mail Queue. Check for DNS resolution delays and upstream server connectivity.
